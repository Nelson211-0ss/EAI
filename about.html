About Us

Who We Are

Positioning Statement

EAI Consulting is a mission-driven consulting firm that blends entrepreneurial strategy with social innovation. We partner with organizations, institutions, entrepreneurs, and communities to design and deliver transformative solutions that drive inclusive and sustainable economic development.

As both a professional consulting practice and a for-profit social enterprise, we serve a diverse range of clients, from universities and development agencies to grassroots organizations and individuals, offering scalable services in entrepreneurship development, rural innovation, and community-based capacity building.

Our Focus

From expert consulting to pro bono programming in underserved regions, our mission remains the same: to empower changemakers through action, insight, and innovation.

Vision Statement

To create a world where every individual, regardless of background or geography, has the tools, knowledge, and opportunities to become an innovative entrepreneur and changemaker in their community.

Empowerment

Providing the resources and support needed for entrepreneurial success.

Global Impact

Breaking down geographical barriers to foster worldwide innovation.

Community Building

Creating networks of support and collaboration for sustainable growth.

Mission Statement

EAI Consulting is a mission-driven social enterprise committed to empowering individuals, organizations, and communities through entrepreneurial action and innovation. We bridge the gap between research and practice to advance sustainable, inclusive economic development, with a focus on entrepreneurship, agriculture, and rural development.

As a for-profit enterprise with a social purpose, we reinvest in mission-aligned initiatives that cultivate leadership, expand access, and create opportunity. By combining evidence-based strategies with grassroots impact, we help build resilient entrepreneurial ecosystems across sectors and regions.
